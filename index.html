Tiramisu Cupcake Recipe

How To Make Tiramisu Cupcakes

This recipe will teach you how to make the most delicious desert you will ever serve. This is a portable spin on a timeless and classic desert. This vanilla cupcake soaked in a coffee liqour with a amaetto mascarphone frosting. Expect prep time to be 35 minutes, bake time to be 15 to minutes, bringing a total time of 55 minutes, making 24 cupcakes.

Ingredients

Cupcakes

  • Sugar: 1 3/4 cups
  • Unsalted Butter: 12 tablespoons at room temperature
  • Large Eggs: 3 at room temperature
  • Vanilla Extract: 3 tablespoons
  • Unbleached All-Purpose Flour: 2 3/4 cups
  • Baking Powder: 2 1/2 teaspoons
  • Salt: 3/4 teaspoons
  • Whole Milk: 1 cup

Soak

  • Coffe Liqueur: 3 tablespoons
  • Espresso Powder: 2 tablespoons
  • Sugar: 3 tablespoons
  • Hot Water: 3/4 cup

Frosting

  • Mascarpone Cheese: 1 cup
  • Heavy Cream: 2 cups
  • Confectioners Sugar:2/3 cup
  • Instant ClearJel: 4 teaspoons
  • Amaretto Liqueur: 1/2 cup

Garnish

  • Unsweetened Cocoa: dutch-process or natural, to dust tops

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. In a large bowl, beeat together sugar and butter until fluffy and light.
  3. Beat in eggs individually then add in vanilla.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Alternate adding the two mixtures together, starting and ending with the flour mix.
  6. Spoon the batter into greased cupcake liners, bake for 20 to 25 minutues, or until cake spring back when lightly touched.
  7. Remove from oven, place on rack for 5 minutes, then tilt pan and remove cupcakes returning them to cool completely.
  8. To make the soak: Mix espresso powder, liqueur and sugar into hot water.
  9. Using a fork, poke tops.Place samll amount of the soak over each cupcake and allow soak to absorb into cake.
  10. To make frosting: Combine mascarpone and heavy cream, whisk on low speed until mix is smooth.
  11. Whisk together confectioners sugar and ClearJel, slowly add to the mascarpone and heavy cream mix, then add amaretto. Increase speed to medium-high and whisk until medium peaks form.
  12. Pipe frosting over cakes then dust with cocoa. Chill until ready to serve.

Enjoy
